## Recording

When I record footage of games that use a pixel art style, I prefer to [[Recording lossless video with OBS|record lossless]]. 

## Streaming

I stream to my [Streamplace](https://stream.place/) instance. I can use Streamplace to stream to Streamplace or use it as a multi-stream service for other streaming services. I currently don’t multi-stream.
