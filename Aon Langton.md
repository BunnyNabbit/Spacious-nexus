*Aon Langton* or *Langton’s dragon* is... a dragon. Although created before [[Aon Pooltoy]], he’s not my primary fursona. Although *Aon Pooltoy* is based on him, it’s a separate entity. Lore wise, *Aon Pooltoy* was intended as a corporate employee gift.

The name “*Langton’s dragon*” is an afterthought. *Langton* named his ant. [*Langton’s ant*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Langton%27s_ant). That sounds lame, why not *Langton’s dragon*? Well, it’s more so silly: *Langton’s dragon* is *Aon Langton*. There’s a word for this; I just don’t know what it was.
